Our Mission

FactorSphere aims to democratize journal impact metrics by providing transparent, community-verified rankings that help researchers make informed decisions about where to publish their work.

Key Features

  • Community-driven journal rankings
  • Transparent methodology
  • Open-source platform
  • Real-time updates
  • Verified contributor system
